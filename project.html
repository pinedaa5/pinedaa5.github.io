Ransomware

Abstract: Ransomware is a malicious software that encrypts user files and demands payment to restore access. It spreads via phishing emails, unsecured remote connections, and unpatched vulnerabilities. Prevention involves regular backups, employee training, and maintaining up-to-date systems.

Botnets

Abstract: Botnets are networks of infected computers controlled remotely by attackers. They are used for large-scale attacks such as DDoS, spam campaigns, and malware distribution. Prevention includes securing IoT devices, using strong passwords, and monitoring unusual traffic.
