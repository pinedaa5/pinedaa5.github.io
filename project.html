Ransomware

Abstract: Ransomware is malicious software that encrypts user data and demands payment to restore access. It spreads through phishing emails, unsafe downloads, or network vulnerabilities. Prevention includes regular backups, software updates, and cybersecurity training to avoid data loss and extortion.

Botnets

Abstract: Botnets are networks of infected computers controlled by cybercriminals to perform coordinated attacks such as DDoS, spam, or credential theft. These networks can consist of thousands of devices, making them powerful tools for large-scale attacks. Preventive steps include using strong passwords, updating devices, and monitoring network traffic.

Malware Detection & Prevention

Abstract: Malware detection involves identifying harmful activity using antivirus software, behavior-based analysis, and artificial intelligence. Prevention includes maintaining strong system defenses, applying security patches, and educating users about safe browsing habits.
