Abstract: Ransomware is malicious software that encrypts data and demands payment for decryption. It spreads through phishing emails or infected links, locking files until ransom is paid. Preventing ransomware involves maintaining backups, applying updates, and educating users on security awareness.

Abstract: Botnets are networks of hacked computers controlled by cybercriminals to perform coordinated attacks like DDoS, spam, and data theft. They spread via weak passwords and malware downloads. Preventing botnets requires strong authentication, software updates, and network monitoring.
